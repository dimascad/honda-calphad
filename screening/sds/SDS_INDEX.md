## Hazard Severity Summary

### HIGH (strict PPE + fume hood required)
- V₂O₅, CoO, NiO, PbO

### MODERATE (standard lab PPE)
- Cr₂O₃, B₂O₃, CaO, SiO₂, TiO₂, Cu₂O, MnO

### LOW (dust mask sufficient)
- CuO, La₂O₃, CeO₂, Al₂O₃, MgO, FeO, ZrO₂

## Notes
- All Sigma product pages have a "Documentation" tab for downloading the full SDS PDF
- Nanopowder forms often carry additional inhalation hazards beyond those listed
- Cr₂O₃ is Cr(III) which is far less toxic than Cr(VI), but oxidation during high-temp processing could produce Cr(VI)
- PbO requires hazmat handling protocols under OSHA 29 CFR 1910.1025
